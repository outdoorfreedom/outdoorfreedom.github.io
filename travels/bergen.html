Bergen Trip





In late summer 2022, I visited Bergen and spent two days immersing myself in its natural beauty, embarking on a delightful small hike. Despite its size, Bergen is a remarkably beautiful destination. I strolled through the center, exploring the charming neighborhoods on foot.



From the harbor, I embarked on a captivating boat tour to explore the enchanting fjords.



On the second day, I ventured on an iconic hike to a neighboring hill (Fløyen)—a gentle ascent leading to a serene lake. The panoramic view of the city made the hike truly worthwhile. I started on this route and continued further up.



I took a few more leisurely walks through the city before boarding the train to Oslo.
