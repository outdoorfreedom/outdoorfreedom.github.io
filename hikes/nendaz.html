Nendaz - Bisse du Milieu





Summary

-  Trail: 6.5km long, 495m altitude change, medium difficulty

-  Starting point: Nendaz village

-  More details: Route



This enchanting hike in the village of Nendaz is a versatile adventure that can be enjoyed either with or without snowshoes, depending on the time of year. Personally, I embarked on this delightful hike in late fall of 2023 and was treated to the extraordinary sight of a vibrant rainbow gracing the skies above the village.

The trail is uncomplicated, tracing along the bisse, which are historic irrigation channels intrinsic to the Valais region. Bisses serve as open conduits, skillfully transporting precious water from mountain streams through daring routes to nourish arid pastures, fields, vineyards, and orchards. Many of these bisses, having stood the test of time, are still in use and meticulously maintained. A multitude of trails accompanies these historical watercourses, beckoning visitors to embark on diverse hikes along ancient pathways.

For those eager to explore this scenic route, the GPX file is readily available on the Nendaz Website. Simply import it into applications like Komoot by following the provided instructions. The trail is generously adorned with picnic areas and benches, providing perfect spots to rest and revel in the surrounding beauty.

For those seeking to extend their adventure, the path leads to Lake Cleuson, a captivating natural reservoir.
