Leysin Winter Hike





Summary

-  Trail: 11km long, 460m altitude change, medium

-  Starting point: Leysin Village

-  Route details: Schweizmobil 988



A delightful panoramic stroll that doesn't necessitate a cable car to access; it all begins in Leysin Village. Leysin graces the southern incline of the Tour d’Aï, perched on a broad terrace offering expansive views high above the Grande Eau Valley.

The winter hiking trail guides you through a charming blend of woodlands and meadows. Unfortunately, during my visit, there was a scarcity of snow. If there is proper snow, expect from crossing of the ski slopes on your way.
