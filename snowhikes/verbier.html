Summary

-  Trail: 7km long, 340m altitude change, medium

-  Starting point: Verbier Lift

-  Route details: Komoot Route

-  Additional details: Gondola Timetable

-  Region details: Region Official Trails



I started my journey from Le Châble, using a lift to reach Verbier. Alternatively, one can directly access the village by car.

Following initially the official Clambin hike, as indicated here, the trail is well-defined with distinctive pink snowshoe hike signs. This leg of the hike presents a moderate level of difficulty.

Opting for less challenge in the latter half, I followed the yellow hike signs typically used in the summer, gently descending back to the village.
