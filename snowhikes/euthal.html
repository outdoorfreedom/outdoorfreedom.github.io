Euthal Snow Hike





Summary

-  Trail: 6km long, 220m altitude change, medium difficulty

-  Starting point: Euthal village

-  Route details: SchweizMobil 816



The hike commences from the heart of Euthal, offering convenient access to parking options situated both here and here.

While the majority of the trail is physically not demanding, there is a challenging segment, notably within the blue circle. I've traversed this path in both directions. Descending the steep section presents the advantage of a stunning viewpoint.



The trail segment near the village lacks clear markings, but its course is somewhat discernible. Along the midpoint of the trail, a welcoming bench invites hikers to pause and relish the breathtaking scenery.
