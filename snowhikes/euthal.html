Euthal Snow Hike





Summary

-  Trail: 6km long, 220m altitude change, medium difficulty

-  Starting point: Euthal village

-  Route details: SchweizMobil 816



The hike starts from the center of Euthal. The closest parking options are here or here.

For the most part is easy but there is a section that is quite steep (somwhere in the blue circle. I have hike the trail twice in different directions. The advantage of descending the steep part is the nice view!



The trail part close to the village is not well marked but it is somewhat obvious to find it. In the middle of the trail, there is a bench where one can enjoy the beautiful scenery.
