Alter Schwyzerweg Winter Hike





Summary

-  Trail: 8km long, 440m altitude change, medium

-  Starting point: Laucheren Parking

-  Route details: Schweizmobil 565

-  Additional details: Webcams





Commencing from the free parking area at Laucheren, you can embark on the "Alter Schwyzerweg" trail. This route does not necessitate any lifts or gondolas.

Predominantly aligned with the ski-touring path, the initial section features a moderate incline, yet poses no risk of falling. While the trail does not present any technical challenges, it demands a moderate level of physical exertion.

Benches are conveniently situated along the route, providing opportunities for rest and relaxation.
