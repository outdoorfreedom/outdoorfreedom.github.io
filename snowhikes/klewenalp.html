Klewenalp





Summary

-  Trail: 3km long, 90m altitude change, easy

-  Starting point: Bergbahnen Beckenried-Emmetten

-  Route details: Komoot Route

-  Alternative route: Schweizmobil 850



My first snowshoe hike for winter 2023-2024. I parked at the Beckenried-Emmetten lift and took the lift to reach the starting point. The view of Vierwaldstättersee are amazing from there. I followed the komoot route but one can do the official Schweizmobil 850. For the latter the signs were partially missing when I was there. Overall, both routes should be technically and physically easy and are short. Perfect for a lazy weekend adventure!
