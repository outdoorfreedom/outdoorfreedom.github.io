Summary

-  Trail: 3km long, 90m altitude change, easy

-  Starting point: Bergbahnen Beckenried-Emmetten

-  Route details: Komoot Route

-  Alternative route: Schweizmobil 850



I chose Klewenalp for my inaugural snowshoe hike for the winter of 2023-2024. I parked at the Beckenried-Emmetten lift and smoothly ascended to the starting point using the lift. The panoramic views of Lake Lucerne (Vierwaldstättersee) from this vantage point were truly breathtaking.

Opting to follow the Komoot route, although the official Schweizmobil 850
As a side note, I found the gondola ticket (even the half-fare) and the parking fee to be overpriced. But it definitely deserves it.
