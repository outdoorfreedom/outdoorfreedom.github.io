Haldi Trail





Summary
-  Trail: 7km long, 400m altitude change, medium difficulty

-  Starting point: Schattdorf Lift Station Uri

-  More details: Haldi Website

-  Route details: SchweizMobil 885




The hike starts from the lift station. There is a parking outside that includes an underground section. The gondola is small and runs pretty much every 20 minutes. Check the Haldi Website for more details.

I started the hike from left as I exited the lift station. There are the regular pink signs in the beginning. For the rest part of the route you will mostly see sticks with red and blue color. The first part is steep and there is little forest area. One has the option to shorten the hike, guided by the usual pink signs. I did the entire route.

During the ascending I walked by a restaurant. In the beginning of the trail there are benches but not later on. There is a bench in the middle of the route that oversees the mountains and the lake. That is where the photo above was taken.

Then, descending follows. A bit steep at first and less later. The last part is not well signed, however it is easy to guess the way. Also, on December 9th, 2023 it was not well covered by snow. Actually, I hiked it without snowshoes.
