Schwägalp Winter Hike

Schwägalp Winter Hike





Summary

-  Trail: 3km long, 120m altitude change, easy

-  Starting point: Parking

-  Route details: Schweizmobil 134

-  Additional details: Lantern Trail

-  Alternative longer route: Schwägalp–Kronberg Trail



I explored a lantern trail (laternliweg) during daylight. The Llternliweg in the Schwägalp nature discovery park guides you through a snowy landscape, illuminated by over 50 lanterns. The path is suitable for all hikers.

For those seeking a more challenging hike, the Schwägalp–Kronberg trail is recommended. This route offers breathtaking views but demands a high level of fitness! After this gentle introduction, the steep ascent begins. The panoramic view extends from the Churfirsten and Lake Constance to Säntis.
