Summary

-  Trail: 12km long, 600m altitude change, medium difficulty

-  Starting point: Langis

-  Route details: Schweizmobil 805



In February 2023, I did the Jänzi snowshoe hike, starting from the Langis parking lot and tracing the 805 route as indicated by the pink Schweizmobil signs. No cable car is required, and the trail was clearly marked in a single direction. However, given the insufficient snow cover in the lower section at that time, I retraced my steps, essentially completing half of the route in both directions.

Despite being labeled as "difficult," I personally found the hike to be of moderate technical difficulty. The path did not present risks of falling or excessively steep segments, although the overall elevation change amounted to 600 meters. The panoramic view from the midpoint was truly breathtaking. Additionally, the trail winding through the forest and along the river was a picturesque scenery.
