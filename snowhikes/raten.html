Summary

-  Trail: 5km long, 170m altitude change, easy

-  Starting point: Raten parking lot

-  Route details: Schweizmobil 750

-  Additional details: Raten website



An easy hike in the canton of Zug that connects Oberägeri and Biberbrugg. It offers breathtaking vistas of Lake Ägeri and the iconic peaks of the central Swiss Alps, including Pilatus and Rigi.

The journey starts at a convenient parking lot situated in front of a mountain restaurant. This hike boasts a gentle terrain with minimal changes in altitude. Passing through partially wooded areas, the trail guides you to a scenic viewpoint known as Bellevue. You can find benches there to pause and soak in the surroundings.

This snowshoe hike is an ideal introduction to snowshoe hiking. It is well marked with the pink Schweizmobil signs and the pink sticks.
